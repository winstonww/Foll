Choose the best food within budget

Share

Feel free to share your ideas with friends using Foll ! Give Foll a list of items that you have in mind for a party and let others members in the party rate them. Don't worry if the item you wish to bring receives a low rating, because all submissions are anonymous!

Optimize

Foll collects the ratings of all the items proposed and renders a list of food that will maximize the value based on ratings under the price constraint specified. If the item you proposed is on the top-rated list, foll will automatically remind you to bring it to the party so you won't forget!
